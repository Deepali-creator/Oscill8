How do I clean my unit?

Unplug the charger base from the outlet and clean with a cloth or tissue moistened with warm water. Submerging the init or using a dishwasher is nor recommended and may cause damage or injury.

How long does my unit need to be charged?

16 hours is a full charge.

Can kids use the adult brush head?

They certainly can, however, the brush head is made for adult hands and may be difficult for them to use.

How do I switch my brush head?

Simply twist and pull to switch it out.

What do I do with the colored rings?

The colored rings are a way for you to identify which brush is yours or you can switch them out to match your outfit for the day :) Twist and pull the brush head and slip on your colored ring and you’re good to go.

What is the subscription plan?

If you choose to select our subscription plan upon placing your first order, you’ll receive a new brush head based on the frequency you select. Our recommendation is every two to three months. You will provide a credit card when you sign up and that card will be billed when your items ship.

Can I alter my subscription plan?

Yes, of course you can at any time. Simply log in to your account and switch the frequency, add additional items to your order or place a one-time replacement order.

How do I know when my next order will ship?

Go to log in, click on orders and click on subscriptions and you can see the next shipment date. You can also choose to alter that date.

I don’t need my next shipment. How do I cancel?

You can simply go to log in, click on subscriptions and click skip next delivery.

I no longer want to be part of the subscription plan, how do I cancel?

We will miss you, but we understand. Just go to log in, click subscriptions and click cancel. We hope you return soon!

I want to order Oscill8™ for a family member but don’t want to set up another account, how do I handle this?

Just log in to your account and order a new set then click frequency for refills. Yay, more bright smiles in the world!

I moved. How do I update my address?

That’s super easy. Simply log in to your account and go to address and click edit address and you can follow the onscreen instructions.
